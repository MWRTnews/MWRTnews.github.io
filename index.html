The issue.

Over the past few months I have been exploring crypto and gaining experience. Through this venture down the rabbit-hole, I have found that one thing was missing, short high-quality cryptocurrency articles. Most sources that I found were either too long, too complex, or very biased towards what they were writing about.

The solution.

That is why I created #MightWannaReadThat. My aim is to write one article a day, about a topic that I am passionate about. Whether that topic is open-source software or decentralized autonomy. This is to help enrich our universal pool of knowledge, and to educate the masses about opportunities and risks involved with crypto.

#ReadCrypto
is our motto
